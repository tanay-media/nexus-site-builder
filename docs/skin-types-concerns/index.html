Understanding your skin type is the foundation of effective skincare and dermatological health. Whether you're dealing with persistent dryness, excess oil, or sensitivity issues, identifying your specific skin characteristics allows you to choose the right treatments and products. Different skin types require tailored approaches to maintain optimal health and address concerns before they become serious problems.

This section provides detailed guides for each major skin type, helping you determine which category you fall into and how to care for your skin accordingly. You'll find specific advice for dry, oily, combination, sensitive, and normal skin types, plus guidance on how skin changes with age and seasons.
