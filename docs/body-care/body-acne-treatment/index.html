Body Acne Treatment

2 articles

Body acne affects millions of people and can be more challenging to treat than facial breakouts due to thicker skin, increased oil production, and friction from clothing. Unlike face acne, body acne often covers larger areas and requires specialized treatment approaches that account for different skin textures and environmental factors. Understanding the unique characteristics of body acne is essential for developing an effective treatment strategy that clears existing breakouts and prevents future ones.

This section provides targeted guidance for treating acne on your back, chest, and shoulders, along with product recommendations and prevention strategies. You'll find detailed treatment protocols for different severity levels, from mild breakouts to cystic body acne, plus specialized routines that address hormonal causes.
