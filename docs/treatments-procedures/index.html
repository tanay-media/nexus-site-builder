Prescription Treatments

Modern dermatology offers a wide range of evidence-based treatments and procedures to address skin conditions, improve skin health, and enhance your appearance. From non-invasive options like chemical peels and microneedling to advanced laser therapies and surgical procedures, today's treatments can effectively target acne, aging, pigmentation, scarring, and numerous other skin concerns. Understanding your treatment options empowers you to make informed decisions about your skin care journey alongside your dermatologist.

This section explores the most common dermatological treatments and procedures available today. You'll find detailed information about injectable treatments, laser and light therapies, prescription medications, surgical procedures, and other specialized treatments like cryotherapy and chemical peels.

  • Injectable treatments for wrinkles, volume loss, and skin rejuvenation
  • Chemical peels for improved skin texture and tone
  • Laser and light therapies for various skin conditions
  • Surgical procedures for skin cancer and cosmetic concerns
  • Prescription treatments for acne, rosacea, and other conditions
  • Treatment preparation and proper aftercare protocols
