Eczema & Dermatitis

3 articles

Eczema and dermatitis are among the most common inflammatory skin conditions, affecting millions of people worldwide with symptoms ranging from mild irritation to severe, chronic flare-ups. These conditions cause red, itchy, and often painful skin that can significantly impact daily life and emotional well-being. Understanding the different types of eczema and dermatitis is crucial for effective treatment and long-term skin health management.

This section provides detailed information about the main types of eczema and dermatitis, including atopic dermatitis, contact dermatitis, and seborrheic dermatitis. You'll also find guidance on treatment approaches, trigger identification, and practical prevention strategies to help manage your condition.

  • Atopic dermatitis (the most common form of eczema)
  • Contact dermatitis and allergic reactions
  • Seborrheic dermatitis and scalp conditions
  • Hand eczema and occupational dermatitis
  • Treatment options and management strategies
  • Common triggers and prevention methods
