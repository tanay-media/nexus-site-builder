Active Ingredients

2 topics · 17 articles

Chemical ExfoliantsAcne-Fighting Ingredients

Understanding active ingredients is essential for making informed decisions about your skincare routine and treating specific skin concerns effectively. These powerful compounds are the science-backed substances that actually work to address issues like acne, aging, hyperpigmentation, and skin barrier damage. Whether you're dealing with breakouts, fine lines, or dull skin, knowing which active ingredients to use—and how to use them safely—can transform your skin health outcomes.

This section breaks down the most important skincare actives, from anti-aging powerhouses like retinol and vitamin C to acne-fighting ingredients and gentle barrier repair compounds. You'll also find detailed comparisons between similar ingredients to help you choose the right options for your skin type and concerns.

  • Retinol and retinoids for anti-aging and acne treatment
  • Hydrating ingredients like hyaluronic acid for all skin types
  • Chemical exfoliants including glycolic and salicylic acid
  • Acne-fighting actives for breakout-prone skin
  • Skin barrier repair ingredients for sensitive or damaged skin
  • Antioxidants like vitamin C and niacinamide for protection and brightening
