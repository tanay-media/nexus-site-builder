Chemical Exfoliants

1 topic · 8 articles

Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHAs)

Chemical exfoliants are powerful skincare ingredients that remove dead skin cells and unclog pores through gentle acids, rather than abrasive scrubs. Unlike physical exfoliants, these products work at the cellular level to reveal smoother, brighter skin while addressing concerns like acne, hyperpigmentation, and signs of aging. Understanding how to use chemical exfoliants safely and effectively is essential for achieving your skincare goals without irritation or damage.

This section covers everything from basic chemical exfoliation concepts to advanced techniques for specific skin types. You'll find detailed guides on alpha and beta hydroxy acids, safety protocols, and product recommendations tailored to your skin's unique needs.
