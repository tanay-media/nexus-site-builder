Contact

Have a question about dermatology? We’d love to hear from you.

We’d love to hear from you! Whether you have questions about skin health, suggestions for topics, or want to collaborate, we’re here to connect with our dermatology community.

Email Us

  • General inquiries: hello@dermat.local
  • Editorial matters: editorial@dermat.local

Response Time

We aim to respond to all emails within 2 business days. During busy periods or dermatology conference seasons, it may take up to 3-4 days.

What to Contact Us About

  • Content corrections: Spotted an error in one of our articles? We appreciate fact-checkers!
  • Topic suggestions: Have a dermatology condition or treatment you’d like us to cover?
  • Expert contributions: Board-certified dermatologists interested in sharing knowledge
  • Research partnerships: Collaboration opportunities with medical institutions or dermatology practices

Please Don’t Send

To help us focus on dermatology-related communications, please avoid sending generic guest post pitches, paid link requests for non-medical sites, or promotional content unrelated to skin health and dermatology.

We value meaningful conversations about dermatology and look forward to hearing from fellow skin health enthusiasts, medical professionals, and anyone with genuine questions or insights to share.
