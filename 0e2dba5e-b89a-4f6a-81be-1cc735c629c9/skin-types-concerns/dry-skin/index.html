Dry Skin Guide

Complete guide to dry skin including characteristics, causes, care routines, and best products for moisture restoration

Dry skin affects millions of people, causing discomfort, flaking, and irritation that can impact daily life. Whether you're dealing with occasional dryness or persistent skin tightness, understanding the right approach to care makes all the difference. The key lies in identifying your specific type of dryness and choosing targeted treatments that restore your skin's natural moisture barrier.

This section covers everything from basic skin care routines to advanced treatment options for severe dryness. You'll find guidance on daily care practices, product recommendations, and how to distinguish dry skin from conditions like atopic dermatitis.

  • Proven moisturizers and ingredient recommendations
  • Targeted treatments for face and body dryness
  • How to identify severe dry skin requiring medical attention
  • Key differences between dry skin and atopic dermatitis
