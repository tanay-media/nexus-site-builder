When to See a Dermatologist

See a dermatologist if over-the-counter cleansers fail to improve your body acne after 6-8 weeks of consistent use. Seek professional help for painful cystic acne, widespread breakouts covering large areas of your body, or signs of scarring. Dark spots and raised scars can become permanent without proper treatment, making early intervention crucial for protecting your skin's appearance.

Professional evaluation becomes necessary when acne spreads beyond isolated areas to affect multiple body regions simultaneously. Dermatologists can prescribe stronger treatments like topical retinoids, oral antibiotics, or hormonal therapy that target persistent breakouts more effectively than drugstore options.
