Prescription Treatments

Medical prescription treatments including retinoids, topical antibiotics, and corticosteroid therapies for various skin conditions

Prescription medications form the backbone of effective dermatological treatment for many skin conditions that don't respond to over-the-counter options. These doctor-prescribed therapies target specific skin problems with proven active ingredients at therapeutic strengths. Understanding your prescription options helps you work more effectively with your dermatologist to achieve clearer, healthier skin.

This section covers the major categories of prescription skin treatments, from topical medications like retinoids and corticosteroids to oral therapies for complex conditions. You'll find detailed information about treatments for acne, eczema, psoriasis, and other common skin disorders.

  • Retinoids for acne treatment and anti-aging benefits
  • Topical corticosteroids for reducing inflammation and irritation
  • Targeted oral medications for severe or widespread skin conditions
  • Specialized treatments for eczema, psoriasis, and chronic skin disorders
  • Antifungal and antibiotic medications for infections
