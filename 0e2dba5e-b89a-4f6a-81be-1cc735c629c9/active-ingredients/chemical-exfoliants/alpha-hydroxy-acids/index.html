Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHAs)

Complete guide to AHA exfoliants including glycolic acid, lactic acid, mandelic acid, and their benefits for skin texture and anti-aging

Alpha hydroxy acids (AHAs) are powerful chemical exfoliants that gently remove dead skin cells to reveal smoother, brighter skin underneath. These naturally-derived acids work by breaking down the bonds between skin cells, making them essential tools for addressing everything from dull complexion to fine lines and uneven texture. Understanding how to properly use AHAs can transform your skincare routine, but choosing the wrong concentration or combining them incorrectly with other ingredients can lead to irritation or damage.

This section covers everything you need to know about incorporating AHAs safely and effectively into your skincare routine. You'll find detailed guides on specific acids like glycolic and lactic acid, concentration recommendations for beginners, and expert advice on layering AHAs with other active ingredients.
