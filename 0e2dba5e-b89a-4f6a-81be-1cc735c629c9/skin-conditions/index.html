Skin Conditions

Comprehensive guide to skin disorders, diseases, and medical conditions affecting the skin with symptoms, causes, and treatment options

Skin conditions affect millions of people worldwide, ranging from common issues like acne and eczema to more complex disorders that require specialized treatment. Understanding your skin condition is the first step toward effective management and relief. Our dermatology experts provide evidence-based information to help you identify symptoms, understand causes, and explore treatment options for various skin concerns.

This section covers the most prevalent skin conditions that bring patients to dermatology clinics. You'll find detailed guides on acne, eczema and dermatitis, psoriasis, rosacea, and fungal infections, plus practical tools like our symptoms checker and guidance on when professional care is needed.

  • Eczema and dermatitis management approaches
  • Psoriasis symptoms and modern treatment options
  • Rosacea triggers and skincare recommendations
  • Fungal skin infection identification and care
  • Age-specific skin concerns from childhood to senior years
